The Rule

Carriers Set The Payout — Up To The CMS Cap.

CMS finalized a 2.48% pay bump to Medicare Advantage plans for 2027, reversing some of the more restrictive structural changes tied up in federal court. Carriers set their actual payouts and can adjust them anywhere up to these CMS-mandated maximums.

Translation: The ceiling is $816 initial for a PA MAPD. If your FMO pays you $600 and keeps the rest, that spread is legal — but that's not "the market." That's their cut. Ask for the grid.
